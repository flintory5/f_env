About Us

Founded in 1990, Farmer Environmental Services, LLC (FES), helps clients throughout the United States and Canada recognize, understand and resolve their environmental issues. Whether testing for the presence of mold, asbestos, lead or other harmful contaminants, FES and its dedicated staff of consultants will find a fast, safe, and economical solution. Located in Edwardsville, Illinois, FES is a leader in the environmental testing industry and has served clients in a variety of sectors, including business, industrial, education, government and residential. No project is too big or too small for FES. Our staff of dedicated consultants has a broad range of knowledge and experience in the construction industry and building systems. If there is an environmental concern, we will find it, help you understand it and guide you back in the right direction towards safety.
